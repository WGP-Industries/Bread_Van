How Notifications Work
  • Notifications are stored in your inbox for up to 50 messages.
  • You can view all notifications by clicking "View All Notifications".
  • Notifications will appear in the notification bell icon in the navigation bar.
  • You can subscribe to specific drives to receive updates about menu changes and ETAs.
  • Arrival alerts require location sharing to be enabled.
